What is this?

@geo_will wrote an article, posing some questions about distance calculations on smart waerables. In particular he was looking at the wonderful Fitbit Surge as it has both stride calculations and GPS capability. The question comes down to using geographic distance vs device calculated distance and teh conundrum of having in effect 2 sources of truth.

At sparkgeo this is the kind of thing we do, a lot. Data. Maps. Analysis. Web. If you need a hand with something, just shout.
